## Introduction

Open structures open information and makes it available to all, breaking down the bonds of platforms and enabling a platformless experience for all users across the Open Web.

## The World's Largest Decentralized Informaiton Network

Open operates the world's largest decentralized informaiton network, structuring Open Information from Web 1,2 & 3 and make it accessible to all.

Open democratizes access to information, enabling permissionless access to Open Information by anyone, from anywhere, at any time, and on any device.
